Crispy and savory soy garlic tofu that always hits the spot. Dice your extra firm tofu and set aside. Combine garlic, regular soy sauce, dark soy sauce, oyster sauce or vegetarian stir-fry sauce, Shaoxing wine, sugar, rice vinegar, cornstarch, and water. Spread cornstarch on a plate. Generously coat your tofu. Over medium heat, add vegetable oil and your coated tofu. Fry on both sides until crispy. Remove and set aside. Back into your pan, add your sauce. Simmer to reduce. Add crispy tofu. Mix to coat evenly. Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds. Remove off heat. Enjoy. Visit Profile for Recipe link. Like and follow for more.
